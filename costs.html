Costs

The cost of GPS 2014 is £2,495 per student and £2,495 for those who apply before December 15, 2013.

This includes:

  • Accommodation for 11 nights
  • Breakfast, light lunch, main dinner
  • 1 group pick-up from London Heathrow
  • 1 group drop-off to Manchester airport
  • Transportation throughout the UK
  • All arranged company visits
  • All arranged cultural activities
  • All classes, workshops and presentations

This does not include:

  • Extra snacks or food outside of arranged meals
  • Transportation from/to any location other than arranged airport pick-up/drop offs
  • Souvenirs and shopping

ABOUT US

Helen Auckland created the Global Paths Summer Programme (GPS) to help students identify their goals and develop the skills to achieve them.
Her aim is to share her knowledge with students to help them make a non-biased, logical decision about what to do post-high school.
