Cultural Experience

GPS students gain a unique combination of cultural experiences:

British Culture

Embedded into the busy work schedule, we ensure that students gain a full immersion of British culture: The London walking tour, Harry Potter, fish & chips, Buckingham Palace, double-decker buses, Oxford, Sunday lunch, Croquet, Houses of Parliament, London eye, Big Ben...... are just some of GPS's British highlights!


Other cultures

Each student is also in charge of teaching the group a fun activity from their home country. Developing leadership and public speaking skills, students gain insight into the different cultures of GPS and the most successful activity coordinator wins a prize!
