GLOBAL PATHS STUDENT PROGRAMME HELPS YOU IDENTIFY YOUR GOALS AND DEVELOP THE SKILLS TO ACHIEVE THEM.

11 days. 4 UK cities. Global friendships. Real-life experience.

July 27 - August 5, 2016

The Global Paths Student Programme (GPS) is an exciting, enriching programme for 15-17 year old students interested in preparing for life after high school. There are three main components of the GPS programme.

Prepare for Student Life

For many, moving to university is a huge change - independence, responsibility, new friends, studies, accommodation, culture and city. It’s a lot to handle and it’s tough to prepare alone.

Read more
Choose the Right University

Your education is one of the biggest investments of your life. Choose correctly and you’re happy, fulfilled, working towards success. Choose incorrectly and you’re miserable, frustrated and unemployable.

Read more
Develop Skills for Success

Success in life is based on more than academic grades: The ability to work with different people, think independently and solve problems means you bring value to the world. The capacity to embrace new, communicate effectively and present professionally enables you to build strong relationships through life.
