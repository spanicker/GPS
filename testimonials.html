What students and parents have to say ...

Teodora Stoyanova, GPS 2014 student, Bulgaria

"The GPS programme is really exceptional. It helped me define my vision and what I want to achieve in life; it gave me a taste of entrepreneurship and how an enterprise can be sustained and developed; to experience university life was unique ; the multicultural experience enriched me a lot, it really made me think and act more like a global citizen!

I was really inspired by the motivational speakers, entrepreneurs, and all the people I've met and befriended during the GPS programme, they truly impacted me! These 2 weeks I spent in England felt as a whole month, that's how busy, entertaining and emotional it was!

Another thing that impressed me a lot was how soon we all became friends, and started even feeling as a family! This programme is a fantastic experience of entrepreneurship, university life, travel and cultures, which all build up the character, enrich the mind and show how fundamental it is to be a thoughtful and globally engaged citizen! Andrea Solis, GPS 2014 student, Mexico

Also talking with employees from IBM, Google and Nestle was great as I was able to learn more about how the real world works and how to best prepare for it. From a business perspective, talking to Cadbury's and Land Rover were also very helpful as one would be able to understand more about how they operate and truly understand corporations better.

Talks regarding our purpose were extremely helpful. They helped me understand how to plan for my future and that is something that people at my age find hard to begin thinking about."
