Experience student life at different UK universities

Advising students on UK higher education can be a challenge. Establishing if a university is academically right for someone can be done from afar but sensing if it is the right culture, community and location is hard to do without some first-hand experience.

With the Global Paths Counselor Programme, you visit a range of universities across the UK. You live in student accommodation, tour campuses, meet current students and explore the cities.

You get a true feel for the kind of student that matches different types of university and city - equipping yourself with first-hand experience to share with your students.


Identify the different UK universities and what's best for your students

The UK offers over 100 different universities varying in quality, reputation and environment. These options can be categorised according to academic level, environment and location.

We carefully select universities to visit to ensure each category is represented - providing you with a complete experiential overview of UK higher education.

Understand the UK University application process

There are some specific rules and tips to the UK university application process and knowing these can be the difference between acceptance and rejection for a student.

Throughout the programme, we offer workshops, led by experts, on topics such as the UCAS process, the Foundation year and Writing a good personal essay.

As well as this, you have the chance to chat informally with UK university admissions officers, to ask them questions and to learn what they look for in their applicants.
