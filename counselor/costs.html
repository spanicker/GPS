Costs

The cost of the Global Paths Counsellor programme is £1,450 per person. If you sign up before 1st March, 2016, you receive a 15% early bird discount so the price is £1,232.

Cost includes:

  • Accommodation for 7 nights
  • Daily breakfast, light lunch, main dinner
  • 1 group pick-up from London Heathrow airport
  • 1 group drop-off to Manchester airport
  • Transportation throughout the UK
  • All workshops

It does not include:

  • Return flight to the UK
  • Extra snacks or food outside of arranged meals
  • Transportation from/to any location other than arranged airport pick-up/drop-offs
  • Souvenirs and shopping

Refunds

When you sign up to the programme, we ask you to pay a non-refundable deposit of £600 to reserve your place. It is non-refundable as it covers the deposits (accommodation, minibus, meals) that we have to make for the course.

If for any reason you cannot attend the programme, we will refund you the full fees, minus the deposit, unless you cancel within 30 days of the programme starting, in which case, unfortunately we cannot refund any of the fees.
