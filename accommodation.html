Accommodation

Global Paths is about giving students a taster of university life. Students live on the campuses of 4 top UK universities:

  • King's College London
  • University of Bath
  • University of Birmingham
  • University of Manchester

Each accommodation is different but generally, students have their own individual en-suite room with a shared social and kitchen space.
