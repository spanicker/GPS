Accommodation

To get the true experience of student life, GPS participants live together in university accommodation at 4 of the UK's top university campuses.

Accommodation varies from one university to another:

  • London School of Economics (LSE)
    Shared room (2-4 students) with a shared en-suite bathroom.
  • University of Bath
    Individual rooms within a flat share. Shared bathroom.
  • University of Birmingham
    Individual rooms with a shared bathroom.
  • University of Manchester
    Individual rooms within a flat share. Shared bathroom.
