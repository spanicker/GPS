GLOBAL PATHS HELPS STUDENTS IDENTIFY THEIR GOALS AND DEVELOP THE SKILLS
TO ACHIEVE THEM.

11 days, 5 UK cities, global friendships, real-life experience!

The Global Paths Summer Programme (GPS) is a 12 day programme for 15-18 year old students from across the world interested in gaining practical insight into the world of work and higher education.

Students live on 4 Top UK University campuses, meet presenters from leading multinational companies and experience how an organisation moves from idea to market.

Taught by leading academics and senior corporate employees, students walk away with:

  • An Individual Career Plan
  • Skills for the Workplace
  • A Cultural Experience

ABOUT US

Helen Auckland created the Global Paths Summer Programme (GPS) to help students identify their goals and develop the skills to achieve them.
Her aim is to share her knowledge with students to help them make a non-biased, logical decision about what to do post-high school.
