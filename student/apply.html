Sign Up

It’s easy to sign up for Global Paths 2016. Here's what to do:

  • Submit your online sign up form
  • Pay your £50 deposit via PayPal below
  • Skype interview - once we receive your application and deposit, we’ll schedule a Skype call with you to discuss your application and answer any questions you or your parents have.
  • Counselor recommendation - we will contact your counselor or teacher via Skype to ask them if they believe you are a good fit for Global Paths.

    • Once we have spoken with you and your counselor, we'll tell you if you have been accepted onto the programme. If so, you will need to pay a deposit to secure your place. The remaining fees will be due by the latest 1st June (1st March for Early Bird offer)
