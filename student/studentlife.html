Prepare for Student Life

For many, moving to university is a huge change - independence, responsibility, new friends, studies, accommodation, culture and city. It’s a lot to handle and it’s tough to prepare alone.

The Global Paths Student Programme is a gentle stepping stone between school and university. It’s a selection of experiences that represent UK student life: Live in student accommodation, make new friends, discover different cultures, take classes and meet university students.

It’s exciting, bonding, life changing and fun. You’re treated like an adult so you develop independence, flexibility and maturity - essential skills for student survival!

Rest assured, you’re in safe hands. Our staff to student ratio is 1:4. Trained and experienced supervisors and teachers are there 24/7 to ensure your safety, comfort and happiness.
