Apply

To apply to GPS 2014, please submit the following items:

  • Online Application Form
  • Application Fee
  • High school transcripts from last 2-3 years
  • One recommendation letter from a teacher or counselor

Please submit your transcripts and letter via email to hrauckland@globalpaths.co.uk

Once we have reviewed your complete application, if it meets our requirements, we will schedule a 15- minute Skype video interview with you. We will email you to inform you of the decision after your interview.
