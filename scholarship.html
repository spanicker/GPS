Scholarships

We offer scholarships to students who are in need of financial assistance and who will bring the below attributes to the programme:

  • Diversity
  • Leadership
  • Cultural perspective
  • Problem solving
  • Unique ideas
  • Positive attitude
  • Fun
  • All classes, workshops and presentations

Include in your application a short explanation (of no more than 400 words in length) of why you should be awarded a scholarship. Email it to hrauckland@globalpaths.co.uk with your application documents.
